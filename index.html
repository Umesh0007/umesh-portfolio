About

I completed an MSc in Civil and Structural Engineering at Teesside University, where my thesis compared residential building design in high seismic zones across India and Italy using software-based structural modeling and code comparison.

I also hold a B.Tech in Civil Engineering from GITAM, where I studied the behavior of a G+6 reinforced concrete building under different Indian seismic zones.

My current role in construction engineering strengthens my practical understanding of site inspection, compliance, structural integrity review, and quality assurance.
